Our Facilities

The institute has been provided with excellent infrastructure and beautiful campus. The campus life shall pivot around the library, the computer lab and the seminar hall. There is a computer lab with Wi-Fi internet access facilities. The ever expanding library carries a large number of Indian and International titles on various management subjects and subscribes to leading professional journals. State of the art facilities for Video Conferencing, English Language Lab and Digital Library are provided for students.
